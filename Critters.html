Critters

What Is Critters

Critters is a VR horror shooter built in Unreal Engine 5. The intention of the game was to strip the player of their vision leaving only their hearing to distinguish where the enemies are located. When the player shoots their weapon, their vision will return for a brief period but also trigger the enemies to move to the player increasing the horror feel.

What I Accomplished

  • Immersive Landscape
  • Directional sound effects
  • Player and enemy functionaliy
  • Point rewarding based on the distance between the player and the killed enemy

Challenges Faced and What I learned

I used this project as my introduction to the Unreal Engine as well as a first for the game genre so the project in itself was a challenge for me. I had to learn many areas within Unreal such as AI navigation, landscape tool, and utilizzing the VR framework. This project was definitely something out of my comfort zone, but it allowed me to get a good understanding of how blueprints and the general structure of unreal works.
