Isles Of Aether

What Is Isles Of Aether

Isles Of Aether is an action-adventure game based in Unreal Engine 5. The primary object the team and I had was to provide the player with a fast-paced and versatile combat experience revolving around unique weapons and spells.

What I Accomplished

  • Coordinate a team of 17 members to ensure successful completion of milestone objectives
  • Created the entire weapon attacking system for player and enemies
  • Created base foundations for the player and enemies such as attacking
  • Assisted in creating the foundation for the AOE type spells
  • Created an auto builder that is desiged to build for shipping whenever someone updates the perforce project
  • Assisted with debuging throughout the entire project aside from the enemy AI

Lessons Leanred

There have been two major lessons that I have learned so far with this project. The first would be learning how to manage a group of this size as well as understanding the strengths that each member possesses. The second was creating a very modular combat system that allowed for a seamless transition between both weapon attacks and spell attacks.
